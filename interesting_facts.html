Only 8 species of bears are extant.
Unlike many mammals, bears can see in color.
The giant panda is actually a bear.
Bears have an excellent sense of smell, better than dogs or possibly any other mammal.
A group of bears is called a sloth.
Bears have two layers of fur. A short layer of fur keeps the bear warm. And a long layer keeps water away from the skin and short fur.
